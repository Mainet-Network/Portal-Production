$MNT is a multichain ERC20-compatible token
The Token that empowers Mainet's Economy
$MNT is Mainet's main token, it plays a vital role in Mainet's ecological ecosystem. With $MNT's support, Mainet ecosystem is able to build trust between roles and components using special mechanism. It empowers $MNT holder different rights and interests as well as rightful authority in Mainet DAO.
What is the Function of $MNT in Mainet?
In Mainet ecology, $MNT plays an extremely important role in all aspects.
Ecosystem Managing
Mainet aims to be a permissionless and leaderless decentralized ecosystem. By pledging $MNT, Mainet keeps a balanced environment between different roles.
Network Protection
To be able to protect the entire network, Brokers and Nodes are required to pledge $MNT to be able to do the their work. They will be rewarded if the jobs are done right or else be punished if they run against the intrests of the system.
Online Payments
You can pay directly with $MNT for products and services from specific merchants and get discounts by holding a certain amount of $MNT tokens.
Fee deduction
Directly using your $MNT to get lower rates of fees if you are a $MNT holder for specific merchants on Mainet. There will be more to come.
