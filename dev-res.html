Mainet For Developers
Join a Rapidly growing developer community
Mainet is an open ecosystem. We welcome all technicians and experts with development experiences to join, contribute what you can to the community, make joint efforts to realize the goal of implementing a completely decentralized e-commerce platform (ShopFi) as soon as possible.
What can I do?
Mainet system provides reserved easy interfaces for developers to connect. No matter what the capabilities of developers are, you can always find places where you can do in the Mainet network. Join us, develop and spread Mainet to the world.
Developer
implementing local malls to maine and serve local neighborhood.
Making your own version of DApps for your family and friends.
Developing additional smart contracts base On Mainet's.
Contributor
Testing Smart contracts and join the activity of bug bounties.
Dev Docs making and multilingual translation of your own native language.
Developing tutorials and learning video to spread the tech.
Ambassador
Actively participating in various activities organized by Mainet.
Volunteer in Mainet's developer community to help people, etc
Spreading and shilling online even offline.
60,000,000 $MNT rewards
In Mainet's token metrics, 6% of total supply tokens are reserved for community developers for incentivize the developer community, for active contributors, developers and ambassadors, depending on the contributions you have done, you will receive corresponding tokens as rewards. For those developers who connects local popular malls to Mainet, they will receive a certain proportion of the transaction share using that implementation additionally.
