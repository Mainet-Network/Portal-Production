What is Mainet?
Mainet is a decentralized protocols that connects traditional e-commerce platforms to blockchain world.
What is a node operator?
A node operator runs a server within mainet infrastructure to provide necessary functionalities to keep the network functioning properly.
How do I get Mainet official news?
You can follow us on Twitter/Medium, join our telegram group/Discord channel, subscribe to our email list at the bottom of the page, or come back to our News page regularly.
What is a Broker?
Broker is a person who plays in the middle between seller and buyer on a specific market platform. he takes the dispatched order and follow system's procedures to fulfil buyer's demands.
Where can I get $MNT token?
$MNT token will be available on DEXes and then will be listed on CEXes, we will keep this page updated once there is a update.
How do I use Mainet to buy products?
Mainet is actively developing according to our roadmap, there will be a chrome extension first available that you can use on vary e-commerce websites, stay tuned!
