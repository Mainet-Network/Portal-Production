-Mainet DAO-
Participate in Decision-making and Create a Better Future
Participate in Mainet governance by submitting or voting on improvement proposals. DAO is an important step towards the decentralization of Mainet. Please come and make suggestions for the future ecological development of Mainet
What is a Mainet DAO?
DAO stands for "decentralized autonomous organization." The Mainet DAO has the most components of a Mainet Important smart contracts and assets,Mainet is a staunch practitioner of distributed governance. Distributed governance is the core strategy for the future development of Mainet project. Therefore, Mainet will be handed over to the community to govern by time, and all stakeholders participated in the discussion to govern the project through proposals and promote the development of Mainet.
What is MIP?
MIP (Mainet Improvement Proposal) stands for proposals submitted by Mainet community members to suggest improvements for the entire Mainet system. All the $MNT holders have rights to vote on them whether it's "Support" or "Against". In the beginning, it will be off-chain, gas-free community. The members of Mainet DAO committee are able t o create proposals and all members can vote for those proposals. Because you care that we progress.
Why should I care about DAO?
Mainet was the first fully decentralized e-commerce platform. Devolution is addressed by separating governance from consensus. In short - you, the user. With the DAO, you can control the policies that are made to determine Mainet's behavior: for example, 1. which countries the DAO votes on prioritize interface development. 2. which platforms should be supported first. 3. Increase or decrease incentive percentage, etc.
How does DAO work?
At present, Mainet core team initiates proposals for policy updates and future planning, and the community uses Snapshot to vote. If the number reaches the specified number, the proposal will be approved, which is not completely decentralized governance. The ultimate goal of Mainet is to be a project completely governed by a distributed autonomous organization (DAO). The vote is conducted on the Mainet Snapshot governance interface. Mainet core team is only taking control in the beginning, overtime, the control will be handled over fully to the community to implement a fully decentralized autonomous organization.
What things can I be part of?
As said above, Mainet DAO will be fully decentralized over time, and the ranges of proposals will be expanded too over time, this includes: 1. Direction of Mainet project. 2. Tokenomic changes. 3. Market expansion strategy 4. etc...
